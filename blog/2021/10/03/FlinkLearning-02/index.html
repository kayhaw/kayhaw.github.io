keyBy和maxBy​

输入数据源

sensor_04,1633264100,36.8
sensor_04,1633264101,37.8
sensor_04,1633264102,35.8
sensor_04,1633264103,38.8

max输出结果:只更新max字段，其他不变

Sensor{id='sensor_04', timestamp=1633264100, temperature=36.8}
Sensor{id='sensor_04', timestamp=1633264100, temperature=37.8}
Sensor{id='sensor_04', timestamp=1633264100, temperature=37.8}
Sensor{id='sensor_04', timestamp=1633264100, temperature=38.8}

maxBy输出结果：打印max字段对应的记录

Sensor{id='sensor_04', timestamp=1633264100, temperature=36.8}
Sensor{id='sensor_04', timestamp=1633264101, temperature=37.8}
Sensor{id='sensor_04', timestamp=1633264101, temperature=37.8}
Sensor{id='sensor_04', timestamp=1633264103, temperature=38.8}

reduce：归约数据，灵活性比max，maxBy更强
